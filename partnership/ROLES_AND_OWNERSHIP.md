# PeakHer.ai -- Roles, Responsibilities & Weekly Meeting Framework
## Jairek (High D) + Amanda (High I) Partnership Playbook

---

## 1. OWNERSHIP SPLIT BY ZONE

### Amanda -- Chief Brand & Community Officer (the WHAT and WHO)
**Zone:** Everything the user sees, feels, and shares.

| Area | Specifics | Decision Authority |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Brand Voice & Identity** | Manscaped/Poo-Pourri energy, tone of coach personas, copy | FINAL say |
| **Content & Social Media** | 12-week playbook execution, posts, stories, UGC | FINAL say |
| **User Experience** | Onboarding flow feel, what content lands, what feels "off" | FINAL say |
| **Community** | DMs, feedback loops, early adopter relationships, testimonials | FINAL say |
| **Partnerships & PR** | Influencer outreach, podcast appearances, brand collabs | FINAL say |
| **Phase Content Direction** | Which nutrition/fitness/emotional tips resonate with REAL women | FINAL say |

**Amanda's superpower (High I):** She IS the target user. Her instinct for what feels right > any data point. Her enthusiasm is the growth engine for community and word-of-mouth.

---

### Jairek -- Chief Product & Technology Officer (the HOW and WHEN)
**Zone:** Everything that makes the product work, scale, and generate revenue.

| Area | Specifics | Decision Authority |
|------|-----------|-------------------|

**Jairek's superpower (High D):** Turns vision into shipped product fast. Cuts through noise to the 1 thing that moves the needle. Keeps the trains running.

---

### SHARED DECISIONS (must align before moving)
- **Pricing changes** (Jairek proposes, Amanda validates from user POV)
- **Major feature launches** (Jairek scopes, Amanda approves messaging/positioning)
- **Hiring or contractors** (both agree on who and why)
- **Brand pivots** (Amanda proposes, Jairek validates from business POV)
- **Launch timing** (balance "ready" vs "perfect" -- D wants to ship, I wants it right)

## 2. THE D/I OPERATING MANUAL

### How to get the best out of each other:

| Dynamic | What happens if unchecked | The fix |
|---------|--------------------------|---------|
| **D steamrolls I's ideas** | Amanda agrees in the moment, feels unheard later, energy drops | Jairek: Ask "What would make this feel right to you?" BEFORE deciding |
| **I talks through ideas out loud** | Jairek gets impatient, cuts to "just tell me the answer" | Jairek: Give Amanda 3-5 min to think out loud. Her best ideas come mid-sentence. |
| **D focuses on results only** | Amanda feels like a task-doer, not a partner | Jairek: Start meetings with a genuine "What are you excited about?" |
| **I avoids hard conversations** | Real issues fester, come out sideways later | Amanda: Use the phrase "I need to flag something" -- gives yourself permission to be direct |
| **D makes fast unilateral calls** | Amanda finds out after the fact, feels cut out | Jairek: 30-second text before pulling the trigger on anything in Amanda's zone |
| **I says yes to everything** | Overcommitted, nothing done well | Amanda: "Let me think about that" is a complete sentence. Sleep on it. |

### Communication rules:
1. **Slack/text for quick updates** -- don't let small things pile up for the weekly meeting
2. **No business talk after 8 PM** -- protect the marriage
3. **"CEO hat on / CEO hat off"** -- use a literal phrase to signal when you're in partner mode vs spouse mode
4. **Disagree and commit** -- if you can't align in 5 min, the person with FINAL say in that zone decides. The other commits fully. Revisit in 2 weeks with data.

## 3. WEEKLY MEETING AGENDA (45 min max)

### Setup Rules
- **When:** Same day/time every week, non-negotiable
- **Where:** Outside the house if possible (coffee shop, walk). Change of environment = business mode.
- **No phones** except to reference this doc
- **Amanda opens, Jairek closes** (I gets energy from starting; D gets energy from wrapping up with actions)

---

### PART 1: PULSE CHECK (5 min)
*Amanda leads this section*

Each person answers TWO questions:
1. **"What's one thing about PeakHer that energized me this week?"** (starts positive, feeds the I)
2. **"What's one thing that frustrated me or felt off?"** (surfaces issues early, gives I permission to be direct)

Rules: Listen only. No fixing. No "well actually." Just hear it.

---

### PART 2: SCOREBOARD (5 min)
*Jairek leads this section*

Review 5 numbers max. Keep it tight (this is where D thrives):

| Metric | This Week | Last Week | Trend |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Active users (daily) | | | |
| New signups | | | |
| SMS engagement rate | | | |
| Social followers / engagement | | | |
| Revenue (MRR) | | | |

No stories here. Just numbers. If a number needs discussion, flag it for Part 3.

---

### PART 3: TOP 3 PRIORITIES THIS WEEK (15 min)
*Alternate who goes first each week*

Each person brings their **top 3 priorities** for their zone. Format:

> **Priority:** [What]
> **Why it matters this week:** [1 sentence]
> **What I need from you:** [Specific ask or "nothing, just FYI"]

This is where you surface dependencies. Examples:
- Amanda: "I'm launching the TikTok series Wednesday. I need the SMS signup link working by Tuesday EOD."
- Jairek: "I'm pushing the App Store build. I need you to write the App Store description and screenshots by Thursday."

**Ground rule:** If your partner says they need something, treat it like a client deadline, not a suggestion.

---

### PART 4: ONE BIG DECISION (10 min)
*Only if needed. Skip if nothing qualifies.*

Bring ONE decision that requires both of you. Use this format:

1. **The question** (1 sentence)
2. **Option A** (Jairek's instinct -- the fast/pragmatic path)
3. **Option B** (Amanda's instinct -- the feels-right path)
4. **What we'd need to know to decide** (data, user feedback, gut check)
5. **Decision or deadline to decide by**

If you can't resolve in 10 min: whoever owns that zone makes the call. Revisit with data in 2 weeks.

---

### PART 5: WRAP & COMMIT (5 min)
*Jairek leads this section*

Each person states out loud:
- **"This week I will:"** (max 3 commitments)
- **"I need from you by [day]:"** (max 2 asks)

Jairek writes these down RIGHT NOW (3 bullet points in Notes app, shared note, whatever). This is the accountability artifact.

**End with:** One genuine compliment about something the other person did this week. (Sounds cheesy. Do it anyway. D/I partnerships die when the I feels unappreciated and the D feels nagged.)

---

### PART 6: BUFFER (5 min)
*Optional overflow or casual brainstorm*

If you finished early, this is Amanda's "think out loud" time. No commitments come from this section -- it's blue sky only. Jairek: resist the urge to turn ideas into tasks immediately. Just listen and riff.

---

## 4. QUARTERLY CHECK-IN (add to calendar NOW)

Every 90 days, do a deeper session (2 hours, offsite):
1. Are the zones still right? Does anyone want to swap or add?
2. What's working in our partnership? What's not?
3. Where is PeakHer in 12 months? Are we still aligned on that vision?
4. Is this still fun? (If not, why, and what would make it fun again?)

---

## 5. THE ONE RULE THAT PROTECTS EVERYTHING

**The marriage comes first. PeakHer comes second.**

If a business disagreement is starting to feel personal:
- Say "CEO hat off" out loud
- Table it for 24 hours
- Come back to it in the next weekly meeting

No product is worth a fight at the dinner table. The business exists to serve your life, not the other way around.
