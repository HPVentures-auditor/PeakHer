## DESIGN GUIDELINES

- **Colors:** Dark navy/black backgrounds for data slides. White backgrounds for framework slides. Accent color: warm coral for emphasis and energy, teal for trust and clarity. Feminine without being "pink." Premium, not pastel.
- **Fonts:** One sans-serif font (Inter or Montserrat). Title size: 36-48pt. Body: 24-28pt. Keep text minimal -- the speaker carries the story.
- **Data slides:** One stat per slide when possible. Let the number be LARGE. Source in small text at bottom.
- **Quote slides:** Black background, white text, centered. No other elements. These are "screenshot moments." Design them to look striking when shared on social media.
- **Multi-domain tables:** Use icons or color-coding for each role (briefcase for business, dumbbell for training, heart for mom, crown for leader). Consistent across all mode slides.
- **Mode slides:** Each of the 4 modes should have a distinct but harmonious color identity that carries through the Cycle of Excellence diagram.
- **Avoid:** Bullet-heavy slides, clip art, stock photos, overcrowded layouts. Every slide should be readable from the back of a room in 3 seconds.
- **Photography:** If using imagery, use real photography of diverse women in professional, athletic, and family contexts. No posed stock photos.
- **Overall feel:** Premium, intelligent, warm but authoritative. Think: the best TED talk you've ever seen, designed by Apple.
