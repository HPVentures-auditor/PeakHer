The Problem

Every productivity system was built for a body that runs on a 24-hour cycle.

GTD. Deep Work. Atomic Habits. The 5 AM Club. Every framework assumes your energy, focus, and willpower reset every morning. That works beautifully — if you run on testosterone, which cycles every 24 hours. But half the population runs on a completely different operating system.

⌨

The Male Cycle

~24 hours. Testosterone peaks in the morning, drops at night, resets. Every day is roughly the same. All productivity advice is built on this.

☼

The Female Cycle

~28 days. Four distinct neurochemical phases. Energy, cognition, confidence, and creativity shift dramatically across the month. No tool accounts for this.

✨

The Gap

Women aren't broken when they can't power through Day 25 the way they crushed Day 12. They're running different neurochemistry. They need a tool built for their actual biology.

We are NOT

A Period Tracker

Flo and Clue tell you when your period is coming.

vs

We ARE

A Performance Engine

We tell you when your best performance is coming — and how to use it.

Scientific Honesty

The 2025 Jang meta-analysis (102 studies) found no robust group-level cognitive differences across cycle phases. Our answer: group averages are irrelevant. PeakHer tracks your individual data, finds your patterns, and optimizes for your performance. Population-level noise disappears when n=1.

The Core Product

The 4-Mode Framework

Four distinct phases. Four different superpowers. Instead of fighting the shifts, PeakHer helps women lean into them — scheduling the right work at the right time.

Build Days 6-14
Perform Days 15-17
Complete Days 18-28
Reflect Days 1-5
Your
Cycle

Reflect

Days 1–5 · Menstrual Phase

Hormones at their lowest. Intuition peaks. This is the time for rest, review, journaling, and introspection. Don't start new things — evaluate what's already in motion.

Build

Days 6–14 · Follicular Phase

Estrogen rising. Creativity and social energy surge. Start new projects, brainstorm, make outreach calls, schedule networking. Your brain is wired for new.

Perform

Days 15–17 · Ovulatory Phase

Peak everything. Confidence, verbal fluency, charisma — all at their highest. This is your window for pitches, presentations, negotiations, and hard conversations.

Complete

Days 18–28 · Luteal Phase

Progesterone dominant. Detail-oriented, focused on finishing. Wrap up projects, do admin, edit and refine. Days 24-28: emotional sensitivity rises — the "DO NOT send that email" zone.
