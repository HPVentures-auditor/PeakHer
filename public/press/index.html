What is PeakHer?

PeakHer is the first Hormonal Intelligence platform. Not a period tracker. Not a wellness app. A decision engine powered by AI that reads a woman's biology, calendar, biometrics, and daily check-ins, then tells her exactly what to eat, how to move, what to schedule, and why.

At its core is Dot, an AI companion who delivers a personalized daily brief every morning at 7am. Dot is direct, informed, cheeky, and slightly unhinged. She adjusts her recommendations based on which of four hormonal phases the user is in: Restore, Rise, Peak, or Sustain.

PeakHer integrates with Google Calendar, Oura Ring, Whoop, and Garmin to make recommendations specific to the user's schedule, sleep quality, recovery scores, and heart rate variability. The platform also includes phase-aware fasting intelligence, task sorting, and a readiness score.

The core insight: Female physiology operates on a 28-day cycle that predictably affects cognition, energy, metabolism, and emotional regulation. 85% of health research was conducted on male subjects. Every productivity system, workout plan, and nutrition guide in the market was designed for a body on a 24-hour hormonal cycle. PeakHer is the first tool that accounts for the other 27 days.
