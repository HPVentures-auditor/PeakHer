Women's performance isn't random.

Every woman has a unique "Performance Fingerprint": a pattern of energy, confidence, and capability that repeats in cycles. PeakHer helps her discover it, then use it to plan her life around her best days.

Under the Hood

How it's built

Simple, modern, production-grade stack. No over-engineering.

🌐

Frontend

Vanilla JS single-page app. Mobile-first. Works offline via Service Worker.

⚡

Backend

Vercel serverless functions. Auto-scales. Zero server management.

🗄️

Database

Neon PostgreSQL (serverless). 15+ tables. Proper indexes & constraints.

🤖

AI
